EMMA DRICCI FOUNDATION
'AFRICA, NOT OF A LESSER GOD'

OUR VISION



To be the flagship of excellence through servant leadership and charity

OUR MISSION



To work with the needy and the disadvantaged African Child in society to enhance capacity for self development and actualisation

Where We Started

Emma Dricci Foundation was established in 2019 with the main objective of giving back to the community through charity and mentorship.
Since our establishment, we have been self supporting, except for occasional support we have received from friends of good will and well wishers.
We believe with the little help we have been granted, we have changed lives of a few and dragged them into the boat of prosperity. We still believe we can lift the African child from obscurity to prosperity.
